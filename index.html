About

I've been on both sides of the table.

Most acquisition consultants focus on sourcing deals and closing them. I focus on what happens next — the 90 days after you sign that define whether your investment performs or implodes.

Over the past several years, I've worked through 7 acquisitions and 5 exits across a range of SaaS verticals — from bootstrapped productivity tools to multi-seat enterprise platforms. Each one taught me something the last didn't.

I partner with first-time and experienced SaaS buyers who want an expert in the room during due diligence, at the negotiating table, and through the critical first 120 days of ownership.
